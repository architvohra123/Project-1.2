Deakin Co-OP Program

With a strong network of over 7100 employers, the university has the largest co-op work program in Australia.

  • The co-op program allows the students to gain work experience while studying at the university and pursuing their course .
  • The work experience enhances your experiences and allows you to apply your learning in the practical work environment. The statistics show that 82% of the co-op graduates from the Deakin University earn 50,000+ AUD post their graduation.
  • Also 96% of the graduates found themselves working within 6 months of their graduation at various jobs.
  • The graduates work at various position for top recruiters like-
    1. Deloitte
    2. Bank Of Montreal
    3. Apple
    4. Johnson and Johnson
    5. Pfizer
    6. Google
  • 92 % of the Deakin University co-op graduates get employed within 6 months of their graduation
  • 82% of the co-op graduates were earning a minimum of 50,000 AUD, two years after their graduation.

Work -study Programs at Deakin University

This program provides students with part-time, on-campus jobs. Up to a maximum of 2000 AUD per term, the program funds 75% of the student’s salary

  • Because of the persisting situations, a limited number of at-home jobs are also being made available for the students.
  • You can apply for the job on the work-study website
  • The salary will vary depending on the position but the students can earn up to 2000 AUD per term.

Leadership and governance

The University upholds a strong governance system that delivers high standards of decision-making and accountability. This ensures our courses, research and community engagement continues to meet and exceed the highest standards of quality.NewCastle's organisational structure includes faculties and administrative units and is led by University executive and management staff.The University Executive is NewCastle's primary management decision-making body.NewCastle’s governing body is responsible for the general direction of the University and ensures that the appropriate plans are in place for NewCastle to achieve its goals.The Academic Board is the principal academic authority within the University, and is responsible for maintaining the highest standards of teaching and research.

Locations
